Frequently Asked Questions

Real answers from our education experts.

What is Fly2Success Abroad?

Fly2Success Abroad is a full-service study abroad consultancy helping students pursue higher education in the UK, USA, Canada, Australia, Ireland, and more — from counselling to visa support.

Why should I choose Fly2Success Abroad?

We offer personalized university matching, scholarship guidance, test preparation, and end-to-end application support with a 5-rated counsellor team and 3000+ successful placements.

Does Fly2Success Abroad charge for counselling?

Your first counselling session is completely free. We are transparent about any service fees before you commit to a package.

What services does Fly2Success Abroad provide?

Course finder, university applications, SOP/LOR review, visa documentation, scholarship assistance, MBBS admissions, and coaching for IELTS, GRE, GMAT, PTE, TOEFL, and more.

Do you provide MBBS admissions abroad?

Yes. We specialize in MBBS admissions in Georgia, Azerbaijan, and Kazakhstan with end-to-end counselling, documentation, and visa guidance for medical aspirants.
