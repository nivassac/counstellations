Frequently Asked Questions

Real answers from our education experts.

What is Fly2Success Abroad?

Fly2Success Abroad is a full-service study abroad consultancy helping students pursue higher education in the UK, USA, Canada, Australia, Ireland, and more — from counselling to visa support.

Why should I choose Fly2Success Abroad?

We offer personalized university matching, scholarship guidance, test preparation, and end-to-end application support with a 4.9-rated counsellor team and 3000+ successful placements.

Does Fly2Success Abroad charge for counselling?

Your first counselling session is completely free. We are transparent about any service fees before you commit to a package.

What services does Fly2Success Abroad provide?

Course finder, university applications, SOP/LOR review, visa documentation, scholarship assistance, and coaching for IELTS, GRE, GMAT, PTE, TOEFL, and more.
